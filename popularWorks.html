The Pluto Tapes

Pluto, after being signed with Monstercat in 2016, broke away from the music industry. He made the decision in 2017 to leave the record label and set up a home studio in Los Angeles and challenged himself to put out a new song each week. He stayed inside for days, working up a storm. In 2019, Pluto Tapes Vol. 1-3 were released as an accumulation of his weekly songs.
